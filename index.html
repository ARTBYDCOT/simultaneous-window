06 — About

The Project

D.C.O.T. · 2018–

Simultaneous Window is a multimedia art project which connects both the experience of walking through a physical space and that of walking through a digital space.

Parting from video and audio archives of daily commutes, DCOT builds a sense of simultaneity between real and digital life on the web. The platform is made up of daily lived experiences of city life in Bogotá.

This web platform contains an experimental cartography on a traditional map. The user is able to enter and walk through a real physical space, browsing through clicks on the contents stored there. All files provide a geolocation text file address on IPFS, building the decentralized web hypertext.

The project seeks to achieve accessibility to public information on the web. The scheme and structure of the project is saved in a document on the decentralized web.
